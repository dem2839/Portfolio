About Me

I am a Houston-based journalist passionate about investigative and data journalism. With journalism training from the University of Houston and data research experience from the University of Texas at Austin, I specialize in uncovering compelling stories about education, infrastructure, and government.

In addition to my education, I gained valuable community outreach experience while working as an AmeriCorps member. I also produced a data-driven story for Community Impact and a non-linear multimedia story for NPR as a Next Generation Radio fellow, I am looking to gain more experience in investigative methods and data visualization.
